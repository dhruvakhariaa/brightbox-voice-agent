Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US, and 5-8 business days for Canadian addresses.

Q: What do I do if my box arrives damaged?
A: Contact support within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the damaged item, and we'll send a free replacement or issue a credit to your account -- your choice.

Q: Can I get a refund instead of a replacement?
A: Yes. Refunds are available for damaged, missing, or incorrect items within 14 days of delivery. Refunds for subscription cancellations are not provided for the current month's box once it has shipped, but future billing will stop immediately.

Q: When does my card get charged?
A: Your card is charged on the 25th of each month, ahead of the next box shipping on the 1st.

Q: Can I change my plan?
A: Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between Starter, Family, and Custom plans anytime from your account dashboard. Changes made before the 25th apply to the next box; changes made after apply to the box after that.

Q: Do you offer gift subscriptions?
A: Yes. Gift subscriptions can be purchased for 1, 3, 6, or 12 months, and the recipient receives an email notification with their first box shipment.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime from the account dashboard under Subscription Settings. There is no cancellation fee, and you'll continue to receive any box that has already shipped.
