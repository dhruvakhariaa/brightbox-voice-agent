This document defines when the support agent should hand off to a human instead of answering directly.

Answer directly when the question is about: shipping timelines, plan pricing, or box contents; standard return, refund, or replacement policy; billing dates or how to change a subscription plan.

Do NOT answer directly, and instead offer to connect the customer to a human agent, when the question involves: a specific order number, account, or personal billing dispute the agent doesn't have access to look up; a request that falls outside published policy (e.g. a customer asking for an exception); anything involving anger, frustration, or a complaint that policy alone won't resolve.

Example fallback line: "I don't have access to your specific order or account details, but I can connect you with a member of our support team who can help -- would you like me to do that?"
